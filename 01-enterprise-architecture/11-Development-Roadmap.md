4. Product Release & MVP Build Roadmap
The InverBrass platform shall be delivered through incremental releases. Each release consists of one or more Build Packs that deliver complete business capabilities. Every Build Pack shall be fully designed, developed, tested, and approved before the next Build Pack begins.
Release	Business Goal	Build Packs
Release 1 – Platform Foundation	Enable businesses to onboard, configure, and activate the platform.	BP-001: Business Setup & Onboarding
Release 2 – Business Operations	Enable businesses to run daily operations.	BP-002: Customer & Supplier ManagementBP-003: Product & Service CatalogueBP-004: Pricing, Tax & DiscountsBP-005: Sales & CheckoutBP-006: Payments & ReceiptingBP-007: Inventory & Purchasing
Release 3 – Customer Growth	Help businesses attract, retain, and serve customers.	BP-008: CRM & Lead ManagementBP-009: Bookings & AppointmentsBP-010: Loyalty & RewardsBP-011: Digital Catalogue & Social Commerce
Release 4 – Business Control	Improve financial visibility, governance, and operational efficiency.	BP-012: Expenses & CashbookBP-013: Receivables & CollectionsBP-014: ReconciliationBP-015: Dashboards & ReportingBP-016: Notifications & Workflows
Release 5 – Workforce & Productivity	Digitize employee and operational management.	BP-017: Workforce & Performance ManagementBP-018: Tasks & RemindersBP-019: Document ManagementBP-020: Asset & Equipment Management
Release 6 – AI Business Advisor	Provide intelligent business insights and automation.	BP-021: AI Business AdvisorBP-022: Business Health Monitoring
Release 7 – Industry Solutions	Deliver configurable industry-specific solutions using the shared Core Platform.	Retail, Restaurant, Salon, Car Wash, Pharmacy, School, Property Management, Chama, and future Industry Solutions
________________________________________
Delivery Principles
•	Build one Build Pack at a time.
•	Complete design, development, testing, and approval before starting the next Build Pack.
•	Reuse Core Platform capabilities wherever possible.
•	Prefer configuration over customization.
•	Every release shall deliver usable business value.
